## Partnership Value Proposition

**For Creators:**
- Permanent embed of their review video on high-traffic SmartReview product pages
- Dofollow backlink to their YouTube channel from each embedded page
- Exposure to SmartReview's growing organic search audience
- Co-branded "Expert Review" badge on product pages
- Optional affiliate revenue share on products reviewed

**For SmartReview:**
- Rich video content on product pages (increases time on page, SEO signals)
- Creator credibility lends trust to reviews
- Backlink exchange — creators link to SmartReview from video descriptions
- Fresh content pipeline without in-house video production costs

## 3. Outreach Execution Plan

### Phase 1: Research & Personalize (Week 1)
- [ ] Verify subscriber counts and recent activity for all 50 channels
- [ ] Watch 1 recent video per creator, note specific talking points for personalization
- [ ] Find contact emails (YouTube About page, linked socials, business inquiries)
- [ ] Prioritize by: engagement rate > subscriber count > posting frequency

### Phase 2: Initial Outreach (Week 2)
- [ ] Send Template A to top 20 creators (4 per category, highest engagement)
- [ ] Send DM (Template C) as backup on social channels
- [ ] Track opens/replies in outreach spreadsheet

### Phase 3: Follow-Up & Expand (Week 3)
- [ ] Send Template B to non-responders from Week 2
- [ ] Send Template A to remaining 30 creators
- [ ] Begin onboarding any confirmed partners (collect video URLs, branding assets)

### Phase 4: Integration (Week 4)
- [ ] Embed partner videos on SmartReview product pages
- [ ] Add "Expert Reviewer" badges and channel backlinks
- [ ] Notify partners with live page links
- [ ] Request reciprocal links in video descriptions

### Success Metrics
- **Target response rate:** 15-20% (8-10 creators)
- **Target conversion rate:** 5-10% (3-5 active partnerships per category)
- **KPI:** Backlinks acquired, video embeds live, referral traffic from YouTube
